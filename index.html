Objective

R4TLI is an initiative that endeavors to promote research and development in the transport & logistics sector in Sri Lanka that would help diagnose its current issues and identify potential improvements based on scientific evidence. This annual research conference intends to build a platform where the needs of the industry can be discussed and scientific processes developed for rectification of issues can be showcases through research presentations

Organization

In this respect the Chartered Institute of Logistics & Transport Sri Lanka (CILT SL) partners the newly formed Society of Transport & Logistics Sri Lanka- a member of the East Asia Society for Transport Studies (EASTS). It will also seek support from the East Asia Society for Transport Studies (EASTS), along with government and private sector agencies in Sri Lanka,

Abstracts and Papers

  • Submission Instructions
  • Presentations / Publications
  • All abstracts should be written in English with a maximum paper length of 2 printed A4 pages including tables and figures. Submissions should be in Word in New Times Roman format to 12 fonts size and 1.15 spacing. All figures and tables should be clear and legible.
  • Papers may be submitted through EasyChair or through direct email to ganga.s@sliit.lk
  • Manuscripts that do not conform to the above formatting guidelines will not be considered
  • We request all authors to carefully revise the paper for language and formatting.
  • Manuscripts should subscribe to the objective of the conference and be based on a topic or research approach that is of relevant to the transport & logistics industry, especially in Sri Lanka
  • Please contact ganga.s@sliit.lk or mahindab@uom.lk if you have any questions about submitting your papers.
  • Manuscripts that do not conform to the above formatting guidelines will not be considered
  • Download Sample Research Paper Format

  • All abstracts that are accepted will be published as conference proceedings both in hard cover and digitally
  • In addition any author who wishes to submit a longer and fuller paper for review and if accepted, will be published as peer reviewed full papers in the conference proceedings. Such papers should be fully referenced and not exceed 12 pages in length.
  • The conference proceedings will be published digitally and steps will be taken to have it suitably indexed.
  • There will be 15 minutes for each presentation and 10 minutes for questions
  • It is recommended for presentations to be limited to 20 slides and to use font size of at least 20
